Civilization is on the brink of destruction, as the "Sea of Corruption" spreads across the land, and humans fight each other for what little land and resources are left. Nausicaa, a young princess from the remote Valley of the Wind, has a mysterious connection to this Sea of Corruption and its mounstrous inhabitants, giant insects that will do anything to protects its territory. After Nausicaa's valley is invaded by a power hungry empire, Nausicaa finds herself in the midst of a war between the last of mankind and nature. The story tackles themes of environmental protection and is seen as one of the biggest influences in Japanese animation.
