“Castle in the Sky” is an imaginative tale full of mystery and adventure. The high-flying journey begins when Pazu, an orphan mining apprentice, stumbles into a young girl, Sheeta, floating down from the sky. She wears a glowing pendant, which the two later discover is a levitation stone linked to Sheeta's past and a legendary floating castle. Their quest won't be easy, however. Air pirates, secret agents, and astounding obstacles keep them from the truth—and from each other.
