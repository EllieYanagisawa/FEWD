The story follows two sisters, Satsuke and Mei, who have moved to rural postwar Japan to be closer to their hospitalized mother. The magic begins almost as soon as they set foot into the old house, where little soot spirits hide in the floorboards, a large but friendly forest spirit lives in the forest surrounding their new home, and a cat-bus traverses the countryside, unseen by human eyes. The two sisters make friends with Totoro and his companions.
